Vegetarian Stir-Fry

Cuisine : Asian Servings : 3 Mode of Cooking : Medium

Preparation Time

15 Minutes

Cooking Time

20 Minutes

Calorie Per Servings

250

Ingredients Needed

  • Tofu, cubed
  • Broccoli florets
  • Carrots, sliced
  • Bell peppers, sliced
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger, minced
  • Garlic, minced
  • Sesame oil
  • Cooked rice for serving

Cooking Instruction

  1. In a wok, heat sesame oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add ginger and garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add tofu and stir-fry until golden.
  4. Add vegetables and cook until crisp.
  5. Add soy sauce and toss well.
  6. Serve over cooked rice.
